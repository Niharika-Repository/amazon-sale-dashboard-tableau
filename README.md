# Amazon Sales Dashboard Using Tableau

## 1. Title
**Amazon Sales Dashboard: Profitability, Revenue, and Order Analysis (Tableau)**

---

## 2. Executive Summary
This project presents an interactive sales and profitability dashboard built using amazon sale.  
The analysis is based on the `amazon_sale_analysis` dataset containing **1,710 records**, covering regions, countries, sales channels, and products for amazon sale.

The dashboard focuses on profitability, revenue efficiency, order behavior, logistics performance, and product contribution to support data-driven business decisions through visual analytics.

---

## 3. Business Problem
Large-scale e-commerce operations generate complex sales data across multiple regions, channels, and product categories. Business teams face challenges in:

- Identifying profitable regions and countries  
- Evaluating revenue contribution by sales channel  
- Tracking order volume trends over time  
- Assessing logistics efficiency and shipment performance  
- Measuring product-level contribution to revenue and profit  

Raw transactional data does not clearly highlight these patterns. This project addresses the need for a centralized, KPI-driven dashboard that converts sales data into actionable insights.

---

## 4. Methodology
The dashboard was designed to evaluate sales performance, profitability, demand patterns, and operational efficiency using targeted business-focused analyses.

The analysis includes:
- **Profit by Region and Country** for geographic performance assessment  
- **Channel Revenue Analysis** to evaluate revenue contribution by channel  
- **Orders per Year** to analyze demand growth and sales trends  
- **Orders by Priority** to assess service demand and fulfillment workload  
- **Year vs Total Cost** to compare cost behavior against business growth  
- **Average Revenue by Year** to track normalized revenue trends  
- **Item-Level Analysis** (revenue, profit margin, shipment days, units sold) to evaluate product performance  

---

## 5. KPIs Considered
The following KPIs were selected to provide a concise view of financial performance, demand, and operational efficiency:

- **Total Revenue** – Overall sales performance  
- **Total Profit** – Business value generated  
- **Average Shipment Days** – Logistics efficiency  
- **Total Units Sold** – Demand and volume indicator  

All KPIs update dynamically based on dashboard filters.

---

## 6. Results & Key Insights
- Profitability varies significantly across regions and countries  
- Certain sales channels contribute a disproportionate share of revenue  
- Order volumes show consistent year-over-year growth  
- Some products generate high revenue but operate on low margins  
- Shipment duration varies by product and region, impacting efficiency  

---

## 7. Business Recommendations
- Prioritize investment in high-profit regions and countries  
- Reassess underperforming sales channels  
- Control costs where expense growth exceeds revenue growth  
- Optimize pricing and logistics for low-margin, high-volume items  
- Use order-priority insights to improve fulfillment planning
